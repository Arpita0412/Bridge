About Us


We are a group of developers who have provided a platform to the local businessmen and entrepreneurs. The increasing demand for online shopping has affected the sales of these local shopkeepers. Though they keep every item that is available online but people still rush to the online shopping products where you may not get the in-hand feel of the product and may end up receiving a faulty product. The main aim of our organisation is to uplift the local artesans and shopkeepers.
